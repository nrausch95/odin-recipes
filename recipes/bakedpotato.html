Baked Potato

A baked potato, known in some parts of the United Kingdom as a jacket potato, is a preparation of potato. It may be served with fillings, toppings or condiments such as butter, cheese, sour cream, gravy, baked beans, and even ground meat or corned beef.

    Ingredients
  • Russet potatos
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
    Steps
  1. Heat oven to 450F
  2. Place potatos on baking sheet
  3. Poke several holes in each potato with a fork
  4. Bake for 25 minutes
  5. Brush potatos with melted butter or oil. Add salt.
  6. Bake potatos for another 20-25 minutes.
  7. Cut open, add seasonings/toppings, and eat!
