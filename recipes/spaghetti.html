Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a delicious, easy to make meal created in Italy. It consists of noodles, sauce, and occasionally meat. It is a staple around the world because of its low effort and low cost.

    Ingredients
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • parmesan cheese
      • Steps
    1. Fill large pot with water and set to boil on stove.
    2. After 5-10 minutes, once water is boiling, place uncooked noodles into water.
    3. After around 15 minutes, noodles should be fully cooked. Once cooked, dump water into sink while keeping noodles in the pot.
    4. Open jar of tomato sauce and put however much you desire onto noodles.
    5. Take salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese and sprinkle onto sauce.
    6. Enjoy your delicious Italian cuisine!
